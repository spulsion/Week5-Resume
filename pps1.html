Project Description

PPS1 is a modular combat system designed for platforms like Roblox and Unity. The goal is to let users integrate 3D models and corresponding sound effects through an intuitive drag-and-drop workflow, making it easier to build and extend gameplay systems.

Skills Learned

  • Modular system design and component-based thinking
  • Working with game-engine asset pipelines (models and audio)
  • Planning user-friendly workflows (drag-and-drop integration)
